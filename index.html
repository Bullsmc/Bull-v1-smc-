What exchanges does it work with?
BULLS v1 works on any exchange that supports TradingView charts — Binance, ByBit, BingX, OKX, Bitget, MEXC and more. You trade on the exchange, the indicator is on TradingView.
Do I need a paid TradingView plan?
No. The free TradingView plan is enough to use BULLS v1. A Pro+ plan is recommended for more bar history and faster alerts, but not required to get started.
Can I automate the signals?
Yes. TradingView alerts with webhooks can connect to WunderTrading ($20/mo) or 3Commas ($49/mo) for full automation on supported exchanges. Setup guide included.
What timeframe is it optimized for?
15m is the primary and recommended timeframe. All backtest data (93 trades, 7.5 months) was generated on 15m. The indicator also works on 1H for fewer but higher quality signals.
Why is the SL rate 45%?
15m crypto has frequent stop hunts — this is normal. The system is profitable because each win is worth 3.5R while each loss is only -1R. Even with 45% SL rate, EV is +0.48R per trade, which compounds to +$888 on $1,000 over 7.5 months.
Which crypto pairs work best?
BTCUSD, ETHUSD, SOLUSDT, XRPUSD, BNBUSD all work well. All backtest data shown is from BTCUSD 15m. Settings vary slightly per coin — full settings guide included with purchase.
